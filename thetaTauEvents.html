Our Events

The brothers of Theta Tau take part in many service projects both on and off campus to give back to the local community and many of the surrounding communities. Our projects embody brotherhood, service and profession as we strive to improve not only our campus and our communities, but also to ourselves in the process as we grow as brothers together.
Some of our past and on-going projects include “Tauquitos”, “Broses”, Splash, Habitat for Humanity, Highlanderthon, Park Cleanup, Speednetworking. Many of these projects were held on campus but span all across Newark and New Jersey in our attempts to better those communities and demonstrate what it means to be a part of Theta Tau.

Theta Tau Events

HighlanderThon

April 7

HighlanderThon is a high-energy 12 hour dance marathon hosted on our campus to benefit the kids at the Children’s Specialized Hospital. The event will be held on April 7th, 2018 where many of our brothers will be participating and volunteering at this wonderful event. For the Kids!

Splash

March 24

Splash is an educational program for high school students who attend classes taught by NJIT students. Many of our brothers taught classes for splash on March 24th and were involved in setting up and running the event as volunteers.

Broses

February 1 & February 5

In order to support our colony’s general fund, the brothers of Theta tau on the days leading up to Valentine’s day sold Valentine’s day cards and roses. On Valentine’s day, we delivered the cards and roses to the listed recipients.

Splash is an educational program for high school students who attend classes taught by NJIT students. Many of our brothers taught classes for splash on March 24th and were involved in setting up and running the event as volunteers.

Initiation Ceremony

Feb. 10

National Officers of Theta Tau came to NJIT on this day to initiate 21 students as a renowned colony of Theta Tau. Starting with workshops that brought the brothers together in teamwork activities, the ceremony ended with a banquet, where we recieved our plaque and flag as symbols of our establishment.
